ABOUT US



Sunnies Studios was founded in 2013, first as a one stop-shop

for sunglasses and later on as a full-service optical that

houses both sunglasses and prescription eyewear. Inspired by

classic silhouettes and contemporary trends, each product is

designed for everyday comfort and pleasure. There’s one for

everyone whether it’s for staying in or going out. We make

sure you invest in your vision with a frame you’ll love and keep

for a long time without ever having to overspend or

compromise quality.
